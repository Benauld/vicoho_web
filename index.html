About

ViCoHo – Virtual Coffee House was set up in 2018 to re-capture the simplicity of raising funds and trading securities in the original coffee houses, coupled with modern and secure Blockchain technology.

We provide access to a fair, regulated & transparent financial investment eco-system on blockchain.

A facility for companies and investors to access capital and asset liquidity, with fewer costs, fuss or third parties. We only allow real securities backed by UK regulations.

The ICO (Initial Coin Offering) bubble

ICO investment is under pressure from regulation. Many have no fundamental real world value underpinning them, leaving investors exposed to loss and fraud. Companies misusing these will have their reputations tarnished.

Major social media platforms and Google, including Facebook, Instagram and Twitter have banned advertising for initial coin offerings (ICOs). Facebook came first saying that such ads are related to ‘deceptive promotional practices’

The FCA’s warning follows a notice from the US financial regulator, the Securities and Exchange Commission, which said at the end of August that some ICOs may be “scams” designed to move a company’s share price. People’s Bank of China, said ICOs were “essentially a form of unapproved illegal public financing behaviour”. ViCoHo offer an OTC marketplace for SME Securities.
